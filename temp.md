#### Section 1: Introduction to Yoga
**Day 1**
- **Main Content:** Introduction to Yoga
  - Yoga is a physical, mental, and spiritual practice originating from ancient India. It combines postures, breathing techniques, and meditation to promote overall well-being.
  - The word "yoga" comes from the Sanskrit word "yuj" meaning "to join" or "to unite."

- **Definitions:**
  - What is yoga?: Yoga is a holistic practice that combines physical postures, breathing techniques, and meditation to promote physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.
  - Types of Yoga: There are nine main types of yoga, including Hatha, Vinyasa, Ashtanga, Iyengar, Kundalini, Bikram, Power, Restorative, and Yin.

- **Examples:**
  - Physical postures: Downward-facing dog, Warrior II, Tree pose
  - Breathing techniques: Ujjayi, Kapalabhati, Bhastrika
  - Meditation: Focus on the breath, visualization, guided meditation

#### Section 3: Types of Yoga
**Day 1**

- **Main Content:** Types of Yoga
  - There are nine main types of yoga, including Hatha, Vinyasa, Ashtanga, Iyengar, Kundalini, Bikram, Power, Restorative, and Yin.
  - Each type of yoga has its unique focus, benefits, and approach.

- **Definitions:**
  - Hatha yoga: Focuses on physical postures and breathing techniques
  - Vinyasa yoga: Focuses on flowing movements and breathing techniques
  - Ashtanga yoga: Focuses on a set sequence of postures and breathing techniques

- **Examples:**
  - Hatha yoga: Downward-facing dog, Warrior II
